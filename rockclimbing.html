Rock Climbing

Rock climbing is one hobby I enjoy doing and it really gets your body and mind moving. It's more then just climbing a wall, it the tests your strength and strategies of making it to the top.

When you rock climb you have colored coded rock like handles to follow to reach the top. Each route is different based on the levels of difficulty and this can help you know what's your skill level.

Types of climbs

There are many types of walls such as:

  • Bouldering
  • Cable climb
    • lip>Solo climb
  • etc.

Bouldering

Bouldering is more focused on technique and strength due from the fact that you have to think about the best route to take and how effiecient it is for you to reach the top.

Bouldering walls are smaller compared to other walls because they test your skills and performance with the use of no cables. Yes that's another difference with bouldering walls they have no cables.

Cable Climbing

Cable climbing walls require gear such as a harness to climb because the walls are very high and require rope to help you stay on.

These walls test you more on stamina since its a long climb up and not that much on skills and technique.
